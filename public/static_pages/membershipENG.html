Membership


The Academy of Nutrition, Dietetics and Obesity Prevention (ANDOP) is a dynamic and progressive professional association of doctors in Azerbaijan. Membership to ANDOP is voluntary.
The advantages of ANDOP membership:
1. Free participation in scientific seminars held once every two months.
2. Access to international scientific literature with updated information (in the Azerbaijani, English and Russian languages)
3. The opportunity of membership in the World Obesity Federation ( WOF) and participation in the international program SCOPE at a lower cost.
4. The opportunity of membership in the European Association Study of Obesity (EASO)
5. The opportunity to participate in the conferences organized by ANDOP at a lower cost or free of charge .
6. The possibility of study at new ANDOP trainings at a lower cost (discount - 10%).
7. The opportunity to participate in various ANDOP workshops at a lower cost (discount-20%)
8. The opportunity to participate in conferences held together with ANDOP at a lower cost. Are there any criterias for membership to ANDOP?
- Participation at least in two trainings
- Payment of membership fee - 120 AZN.

Note: A membership card with a unique code is submitted to each member of ANDOP. The membership card is valid for 1 year.
